About Us

Platt's Barbershop has been established since the early 1980's. We are a father and son operated business. At Platt's Barbershop we have combined more than 50 years of barbering experience. We are nested in a community in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. We take pride in providing premium haircuts and razor close shaves. We have prided our business on customer service with a down home feel. We are accepting walk-ins.
